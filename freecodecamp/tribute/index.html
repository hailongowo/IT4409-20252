Defining Eras

  • 2003: The Chosen One - Drafted #1 overall by his hometown Cleveland Cavaliers.
  • 2010: The Heatle Era - Two back-to-back championships, two MVP awards.
  • 2016: The Promise Kept - "Cleveland, this is for you!" The greatest comeback in NBA Finals history.
  • 2020: The Laker Legacy - Restoring the purple and gold to glory and breaking the all-time scoring record.
