Yamaha PACIFICA611V BTR LTD

Faithfully recreating the custom-specified Pacifica purchased by Hitori Gotoh in episode 12 of the anime BOCCHI THE ROCK！, this limited-edition model comes complete with a logo-embroidered gig bag and an official certificate of authenticity.

  • Seymour Duncan Custom 5 TB-14 and SP90-1n pickups
  • Graph Tech TUSQ nut and string trees
  • Grover locking tuners
  • Wilkinson VS50 vibrato bridge
  • Alder body with gloss polyurethane top
  • Maple neck with rosewood fingerboard and satin finish
  • Push-pull coil-split switch for additional tonal options
  • Anime "BOCCHI THE ROCK！" Logo-Embroidered Gig Bag included
  • Official Certificate of Authenticity included
